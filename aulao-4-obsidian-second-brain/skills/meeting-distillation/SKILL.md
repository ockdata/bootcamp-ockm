# Meeting Distillation

Use this skill to transform messy meeting material into usable execution records.

## Goal

Produce meeting notes that are compact, scannable, and linked into the rest of the vault.

## Workflow

1. Read the meeting source note or transcript.
2. Identify:
   - date
   - participants
   - project or initiative
   - decisions
   - open questions
   - next actions
3. Create or update a note in `4 - Meetings/`.
4. Link participants to `1 - Stakeholders/` when those notes exist or should exist.
5. Link project context to `2 - Projects/`.
6. Promote durable insights to `3 - Knowledge/` only if they generalize beyond the meeting.

## Default Structure

- title
- date
- participants
- context
- decisions
- action items
- open questions
- related links

## Constraints

- Do not mix durable knowledge and meeting chronology in the same paragraph.
- Keep meeting notes factual; do not invent agreement or certainty.
- If follow-ups are vague, mark them as open instead of assigning false ownership.
